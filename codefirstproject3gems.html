Discover Your Star Sign

Enter your date of birth to discover your Star Sign



ARIES

March 21- April 19

Individuals whom fall in this category are creative, adaptive and insightful. They can be strong-willed and spontaneous. Aries people can also be ambitious and driven to reach their goals which can sometimes mean they are over achievers. With this person, comes their fiery sense of presence when angered. This can also shine through when they are very passionate. This signs also holds great sense of humour and is the one that will get along with everyone at a party. But one thing Aries are known for and that is for their impatience.

TAURUS

April 20- May 20

This sign is all about strength, willpower and stamina. They are very stubborn in their character and they will stand their ground when they feel they need to. Another side of Taurus shows their loving and sympathetic and appreciative aspect. Taurus individuals can be very understanding people in which other individuals feel comfortable in sharing their deepest worries and fears with them. They can also be very patient, efficient and practical. This makes them suited towards job roles in business or becoming a teacher or an instructor. They are definitely one for love, time giving and generosity.

GEMINI

May 21- June 20

Geminis are known for their flexibility, balance and adaptability. They are very quick are grasping concepts and will act upon it. They will tend to have two sides to them and are sometimes hard to predict. Sometimes they can turn from hot to cold and can have very visible mood swings. Geminians can show their imaginative and affectionate characteristic and are very supportive in their nature. It is common that they will normally find themselves in sales, promotions and advertisement.



CANCER

June 21- July 22

Cancerians are family people and are usually traditional in their way of thinking, enjoying the aspect of operating on a fundamental level. They are in love with history and are fascinated about the beginning of things. These individuals are very conservative and can easily hide their moods from the public. Known for being fickle. Besides this, Cancerians tend to be loyal and sympathetic.

LEO

July23-August 22

Leo’s are natural born leaders and tend to be very vocal about their opinions. Very truthful to their words, they are often always correct in his/her statements. They are very good are analysing a situation and are then able to form a judgement, providing positive outcomes for the better. Leo individuals are brave, intuitive, head-strong and wilful. Beneath all this lies their generosity and loving nature. They do not tend to share that easily with others and can sometimes be sensitive at heart.

VIRGO

August 23- September 22

Virgo’s are keen beans and are very pleasant to chat with because of their charm and persuasiveness. They are very inquisitive and has the talent to draw information out from people, making them naturally intuitive. Virgo’s can be highly intelligent, analytical and are able to freely express their opinions. They are excellent teammates and can work well with others. On the other hand, they may be short tempered, impatient and self-serving.

LIBRA

September 23- October 22

Libra’s are peacemakers and often surround themselves with the beauty, balance and stability. However, sometimes they will go to far lengths to reach their goals and it can become unreasonable or unhealthy. Libra’s personality can be caring, understanding and the champion of underdogs. With that, they often do not give themselves enough credit for what they do. Their quiet and shy personality can mean they often need persuasion to come out of their shell. Despite that, they can make out to be excellent debaters.

SCOPIO

October 23- November 21

They are often misunderstood. However, they are often good are executing projects with a cool and confident vibe. Scorpios can often achieve the unexpected when they put their mind to a task. On the other hand, they are often quite secretive in their actions and always observing behind their withdrawn manner.

SAGGITARIUS

November 22- December 21

Sagittarius is very similar to Scorpio in that they have a great ability to focus and can be very intense. They are not patient and do always expect quick results. When failing, they often make extreme comebacks when least expected. This sign are known for forming great relationships with lovers and will have loyal friends. However, they do not handle commitment well and refuse to be tied down.

CAPRICON

December 22- January 19

The individual that holds this sign can be very intelligent, applying their knowledge to practical matters, striving to maintain stability and order. They are often well organised, always aiming to achieve their goals in a systematic way. However, when it comes to receiving criticism, they do not take it well. Capricorn’s are patient and persevering, knowing they will succeed in a task when following a step by step process. With, they do not tend to share their own problems with other people but themselves seem to take on their problems.

AQUARIUS

January 20-February 18

Simple and unassuming, accomplishing goals in quiet and unorthodox methods. Honest, loyal and highly intelligent beings, they are also very easy going and can easily make friendly relationships. However, Aquarius people can be prone to sloth and laziness. Also prone to philosophical thoughts, making them great in the artistic and poetic field of work.

PISCES

February 19- March 20

Pisces people tend to deal with vast amounts of knowledge and is sometimes not known to others. They like to keep a low profile, showing their honest, unselfish, trustworthy side to the people they trust. They can be overly cautious and very gullible too, which can make Pisces very easy to be ‘taken advantage of’ by others. They often hold the gentle and generous title amongst the signs. They can become very passionate about something they choose to set their mind to because of simply their pure determination and dedication.

Astrology In Detail

There is a lot more to your star sign



Leo (♌) (Greek: Λέων, Leōn), is the fifth astrological sign of the zodiac, originating from the constellation of Leo. It comes after Cancer and before Virgo. The traditional Western zodiac associates Leo with the period between July 23 and August 22, and the sign spans the 120th to 150th degree of celestial longitude.Leo is a fixed sign along with Taurus, Scorpio, and Aquarius. Under the tropical zodiac, the Sun transits this area on average between July 23 and August 22 each year, and under the sidereal zodiac, the Sun currently transits this area from approximately August 16 to September 15. The symbol of the lion is based on the Nemean lion, a lion with an impenetrable hide. It is a northern sign and its opposite southern sign is Aquarius.
